Anaguta To The World

The Anaguta people, a tribe steeped in history and tradition, are the custodians of a rich cultural legacy in Plateau State, Nigeria. Known as the "people of bow and arrow".

About us

Anaguta Tradomodern

The Anaguta have a unique identity shaped by their traditional farming practices, love, family, warrior spirit, hunting prowess, food, dance, and captivating storytelling.


This powerful heritage is a treasure worth sharing, and Anaguta Tradomodern is committed to promote Anaguta to the world.
