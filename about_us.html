Purpose & Direction

Why We Exist

Anaguta Tradomodern is a cultural movement dedicated to preserving and promoting the identity of the Anaguta people of Plateau State, Nigeria.

🎯

Our Mission

To document, promote, and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of the Anaguta people through storytelling, community engagement, and modern creative expression — so that no tradition is ever lost to time.

🌍

Our Vision

A world where the Anaguta heritage is globally recognised, deeply respected, and proudly carried by every generation — from the hills of Plateau State to every corner of the earth.

The Story

How It All Began

Anaguta Tradomodern was born out of a deep desire to answer a question asked by many young Anaguta people: "Who are we, really?" — a question that deserved more than silence.

Founded by a group of passionate sons and daughters of the Anaguta land, the organisation set out to bridge the gap between the richness of the past and the possibilities of the present.

Through cultural events, digital storytelling, archival projects, and community outreach, we have been building a living record of Anaguta identity — one story at a time.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

01

Cultural Pride

We celebrate our identity unapologetically — our language, food, festivals, and traditions are a source of strength, not shame.

02

Community First

Every decision we make is grounded in the wellbeing and upliftment of the Anaguta community, both at home and in diaspora.

03

Authentic Storytelling

We tell our stories ourselves — with honesty, depth, and the nuance that only those who live it can bring.

04

Preservation

We are archivists of living culture — recording, protecting, and passing on traditions that might otherwise fade.

05

Innovation

We honour the old by embracing the new — using modern tools, platforms, and design to amplify ancient voices.

06

Unity

We believe in the power of a people united by shared roots — across villages, cities, borders, and generations.
